<analysis>**original_problem_statement:**
Project: Mobile-first Small Group Learning Platform for one church (v1), now named The Room.
The app's value proposition is: “A weekly discipleship hub: meet live, share resources, discuss, and follow up.”

**Core User Flow:**
The app must be lesson-centric, revolving around a Now / Next / After experience on a Lesson page.
- **NOW:** Join Live (Zoom link) + Live Chat.
- **NEXT:** Watch Replay (YouTube embed) + Teacher Notes.
- **AFTER:** Slides/Resources + Discussion prompt + Reading plan + Attendance tracking.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
- **Users & Roles:** Admin, Teacher, Member. Admins approve users. Teachers manage content. Members participate.
- **Onboarding & Auth:** Email/password signup with admin approval required for access.
- **Lesson-Centric Flow:** Everything revolves around the lesson page. The dashboard should direct users to the current lesson.
- **Slide/Resource Workflow:** Teachers upload PPT/PDFs. Members can preview in-app or download. Teachers can reorder resources and mark one as the primary deck.
- **Discipleship Discussion:** Each lesson has structured teacher prompts. Members respond under each prompt. Teachers can pin responses and mark them with a status (e.g., answered).
- **Attendance:** Members can manually mark I attended. Teachers can view attendance reports per lesson/course.
- **Safety & Privacy:** No member-to-member DMs. A private Message Teacher feature is allowed. Content is only visible to approved members of the church.

**User's preferred language**: English
